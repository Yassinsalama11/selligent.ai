Connect your WooCommerce store to the ChatOrAI AI Revenue Operating System.

== Description ==

ChatOrAI Chat & Sync does three things:

1. **Injects the ChatOrAI live chat widget** into every page of your store — one script tag, zero code required.
2. **Syncs your product catalog** (name, price, stock, images, variants) to ChatOrAI every 6 hours and in real-time on product changes.
3. **Syncs shipping zones and active coupons** so the AI assistant can quote accurate prices and delivery times in conversations.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `airos-chat` folder to `/wp-content/plugins/`.
2. Activate the plugin in *Plugins > Installed Plugins*.
3. Go to *ChatOrAI Chat* in the WordPress admin menu.
4. Enter your **API Key** and **Tenant ID** from your ChatOrAI dashboard.
5. Click **Save Changes** — the widget will appear immediately.
6. Click **Sync Now** to push your catalog to ChatOrAI.
